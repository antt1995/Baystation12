21 June 2025

Banditoz updated:

  • Added intent-based actions to card decks. You can use harm intent to deal a new card face up while clicking on something, and similarly grab intent to deal a card face down. Both intents let you deal to a hand of cards you can interact with.

Cakey updated:

  • Stars will now generate for sectors on the overmap, ranging from supergiants to white dwarves. Distance to stars as well as the star class' luminosity will affect the efficiency solar panels.
  • Ports cooking appliances from Aurora. Toggle them on via ctrl-click. Open ovens via alt-click. Click on them to add and remove cooking implements and ingredients.
  • Added cooking implements, to be used with cooking appliances. Click on them with ingredients to prepare recipes, similar to how you would have done with a microwave.
  • Cooking now has to be done at different cooking appliances depending on the recipe.
  • Food can be batch cooked depending on recipe if multiples of the ingredients are added to appliances when cooking.
  • Microwaves have been turned into an appliance type.
  • Cooking containers (formerly trays) can now click on specific items to scoop them up.
  • Cooking containers (formerly trays) can now scoop up reagents from reagent containers directly.
  • Most recipes using eggs now require egg yolk instead. You now have to decide to place or crack eggs into containers using help intent to place, and any other intent to crack.
