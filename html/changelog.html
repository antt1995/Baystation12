15 June 2025

Cakey updated:

  • Awards are now classed under their own loadout category, and have been split up into individual awards to allow the selection of multiple awards from their respective categories.

12 June 2025

Neonvolt updated:

  • updated pipe meter sprite

SierraKomodo updated:

  • You can now cancel your own timed actions by pressing ESCAPE. No more needing to do the "Wrong tool shuffle" in the OR. (This probably still has a chance of doing the extra fail state stuff though, be warned)

10 June 2025

Cakey updated:

  • Coffee cups now have more representative onmob sprites.

08 June 2025

Banditoz updated:

  • You can now shake pill bottles to make noise by either control clicking the pill bottle, or through a verb.
  • Most eligibility checks can now be bypassed when forcing someone to be an antagonist.
  • The antag skill selection UI has been reworked. You now press buttons on the skill table, like in the character setup menu, instead of a separate list pop-up.
  • In the admin skill selection menu, the selected level's proceeding lesser levels are now filled in with green, like in the character setup menu.
  • You can now dissolve pills from the loaded pill bottle into the ChemMaster's buffer.

Cakey updated:

  • Paint sprayers can now select to paint both primary and stripe layers for walls at the same time.

Gfitkiilt & Neonvolt updated:

  • Added new "spy station" away site.

Glloyd updated:

  • Lights now respect lighting mode (standard, emergency, etc) as well as bulb color.

LordNest updated:

  • Vox hull now painted in their signature colour once again

Mucker updated:

  • Stealthed admins no longer count towards the lobby player count.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Construction and deconstruction steps for windows now have timers.
  • Removed an errant pixel from the north-facing sprite for Bun Uniter hairstyles.

Spookerton updated:

  • Mint oil shows up with the correct name in booze-o-mats.

14 May 2025

Banditoz updated:

  • Borgs can now click on Show Radio Channels under Silicon Commands to see what channels they have available, a la headsets.

JebediahTechnic updated:

  • Added windows to deck four catwalk access so all can witness the glory of the catwalks.
  • Removed floating/window lights from deck four, no magic allowed.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • The supermatter alarm is now yellow, to differentiate it from orange alert.

emmanuelbassil updated:

  • Nitrous oxide gas (anesthetic tank) sleeping effect wears off quickly once source of gas stopped; unlike previous behavior where it needs a very long time to wear off.
  • Nitrous oxide gas does not put you to sleep reliably unless a sufficiently high pressure source of NO2 is constantly delivered (1ATM for anesthetic tank which is an O2/N2O mix)
  • Will no longer wake patient on neural suppressor up by shaking them once by mistake.
  • Changeling non-lethal absorb no longer puts you to sleep for 15 minutes; now lasts for about 40 seconds

emmanuelbassil updated:

14 April 2025

emmanuelbassil, Azlan updated:

  • Added recipes for frosted donuts; no longer depending on random chance for them to be cooked.
  • Made chaos donut significantly more chaotic; check the wiki for more details. Sprite by Azlan.
  • Added soy versions of dough, flat dough, dough slice, spaghetti, batter, cake batter, and bread. They can be substituted in all relevant recipes.
  • Modified almost all recipes and their nutritional value to make them more consistent with each other.
  • Alcohol now has some nutritious value instead of none. It also replenishes some hydration; but not as well as water.
  • Alcohol now has sugar content; to the great chagrin of Unathi.
  • Juices now have very little nutritious value instead of none; from 10-40% of baseline nutriment depending on the juice.
  • Now able to frost any donut at any time by simply adding some sprinkles.
  • Added new microwave meat burger recipe (Meatball + Bun).
  • Spaghetti recipes can now be made with finished boiled spaghetti as an ingredients instead of water/raw spaghetti
